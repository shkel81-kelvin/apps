## Overview
The Project Calendar provides a visual timeline of all your project notes, integrated directly with your project schedule. It allows you to see meetings, issues, decisions, and action items in a familiar monthly grid view.

## ‚ú® Key Features

### 1. **Automatic Note Integration**
- Every note you create in the **Notes** tab automatically appears on the Calendar.
- Notes are placed based on their "Date" field.

### 2. **Color-Coded Events** üé®
Events on the calendar follow the same color scheme as your notes for instant recognition:
- **General**: Gray
- **Meeting**: Blue
- **Issue**: Red
- **Decision**: Green
- **Action Item**: Amber
- **Planning**: Purple

### 3. **Project Range Awareness** ‚è≥
- The calendar header displays the overall **Project Range** (Start Date to Handover Date).
- This helps you keep track of where you are in the project lifecycle.

### 4. **Clean, Modern Interface**
- **Monthly View**: A clear grid showing the entire month.
- **Today Button**: Quickly jump back to the current date.
- **Navigation**: Easy Month/Year switching.
- **Responsive Grid**: Adapts to show multiple notes per day.

## üìã How to Use

1.  **Set Project Dates**: Ensure your project has a "Start Date" and "Handover Date" set in the Schedule tab.
2.  **Create Notes**: Go to the **Notes** tab and add notes. Make sure to select the relevant **Date**.
3.  **View Calendar**: Switch to the **Calendar** tab.
    - You will see your notes populated as colored bars on their respective dates.
    - Hover over any event to see the full title and category.

## üí° Use Cases

- **Meeting Schedule**: Quickly see all past and upcoming client meetings (Blue).
- **Issue Timeline**: Visualize when issues occurred and track their frequency (Red).
- **Deadline Tracking**: Use "Action Item" notes (Amber) to mark specific deadlines on the calendar.
- **Milestone Review**: See "Decision" notes (Green) to review when key approvals were made.
